Frequently Asked Questions

What is Digit2AI?
Digit2AI is a technology holding company that builds full-stack, AI-native platforms for small and medium businesses. We currently operate 13 live platforms across 12 industry verticals, each powered by shared AI infrastructure including voice agents, CRM, and automated operations.
What problem does Digit2AI solve?
33 million U.S. small businesses still run on phone calls, spreadsheets, and manual processes. AI adoption among SMBs is under 15% -- most solutions are too expensive, too complex, or built for enterprises. Digit2AI builds affordable, industry-specific AI platforms that give small businesses the same automation capabilities as large corporations.
What is Digit2AI's business model?
We generate revenue through three channels: SaaS subscriptions (monthly recurring revenue from each platform), usage-based fees (per-call and per-message charges on AI voice agents), and vertical licensing (white-labeling industry-specific platforms across regions).
Why does Digit2AI have so many different products?
Each industry has unique workflows, terminology, and regulatory requirements. A generic AI tool cannot serve a dairy plant the same way it serves a martial arts school. By building vertical-specific platforms on shared infrastructure, we launch new products 60% pre-built -- faster than anyone else -- while creating deep switching costs in each market.
What industries does Digit2AI serve?
We serve 12 industries across 13 platforms: professional services (RinglyPro), motorsports media (TunjoRacing), dairy and agritech (Lactotec), martial arts (KanchoAI), martial arts community (Ronin Brotherhood), lawn care (LawnCopilot), wellness and recovery (Recovery + Nutrition), restaurant ordering (OrderGoPro), food and beverage wholesale (Doctor Picante), creative services (PixlyPro), automotive tech (Enruta), AI retail monitoring (AIA Store), and hospitality recruitment (AIHotelTalent).
Why is now the right time to invest?
Four converging forces create a unique window: AI inference costs dropped over 90% in 18 months making SMB AI economically viable; SMB AI adoption jumped from under 5% to 15%+ and is accelerating; voice AI has gone mainstream with consumers expecting 24/7 AI response; and we are first-movers in verticals where no one else is building.
What are Digit2AI's competitive advantages?
Speed to market (new verticals launch in weeks, not months thanks to shared infrastructure), bilingual from day one (English + Spanish unlocking 62M+ Hispanic Americans and all of LATAM), deep vertical lock-in (industry-specific platforms create high switching costs), and lean operations (13 platforms built by a small AI-augmented team with low burn and high output).
What is the company's current status?
Digit2AI has 13 production platforms live and serving real customers across 12 industries. We have 3 AI voice agents (Rachel, Ana, and Lina) handling calls 24/7 in English and Spanish. New platforms deploy rapidly through our fully automated CI/CD pipeline, and our multi-language support is built in from day one.
What makes Digit2AI different from other AI companies?
Most AI startups target enterprise clients with horizontal products. We focus exclusively on underserved SMBs with industry-specific, full-stack platforms. Our shared infrastructure lets us enter new verticals at 60% completion, our bilingual capability opens the entire LATAM market, and our proven execution -- 13 live platforms -- demonstrates we ship product, not pitch decks.

What is the long-term vision?
