Frequently Asked Questions

‚ùì How do I download the iOS app?
Search for "RinglyPro CRM" in the Apple App Store on your iPhone or iPad, or visit the App Store link on our website. The app is free to download.
‚ùì I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
Click "Forgot Password" on the login screen, or visit https://aiagent.ringlypro.com/forgot-password/a>. Enter your email address and we'll send you a reset link.
‚ùì How do tokens work?
Tokens are used for AI features like voice assistants, content generation, and AI images. Each account starts with 100 free tokens. You can purchase more or subscribe to a monthly plan for automatic token allocation.
‚ùì Can I use RinglyPro on multiple devices?
Yes! Your account works on iOS, web browsers, and any device. All your data syncs automatically across devices.
‚ùì How do I set up the AI voice assistants?
Navigate to Settings ‚Üí Inbound Lina ‚Üí Configure your phone number, greeting message, and business hours. Rachel and Lina will automatically handle incoming calls based on your settings.
‚ùì Is my data secure?
Yes! We use end-to-end encryption, are HIPAA compliant, and follow industry best practices for data security. Your data is stored securely in the cloud with regular backups.
‚ùì Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from Settings ‚Üí Billing. You'll retain access until the end of your billing period.
‚ùì The iOS app won't load my dashboard. What should I do?
First, check your internet connection. Then try: 1) Pull down to refresh, 2) Force close and reopen the app, 3) Log out and log back in. If the issue persists, contact info@digit2ai.com.
