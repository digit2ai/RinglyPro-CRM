Source content for the seed script. Each project has: name, description,
feasibility, risk_level, risk_notes, contacts_notes, and 3-5 questions.

## Project 1 -- Website & Client Acquisition (iqbiz.net)

**Description:** Modernize positioning, messaging, and conversion structure
before driving traffic, so the site captures and qualifies leads. Reuse
chamber cookie-cutter template, RinglyPro landing patterns, and
Visionarium / HISPATEC playbooks.

**Feasibility:** HIGH
**Risk:** LOW
**Risk notes:** Mostly content + IA + funnel work; no new platform code.

**Contacts notes:**
- Larry (domain owner)
- Copy / design support
- Target-buyer ICP confirmation

**Questions:**
1. Who owns iqbiz.net today, and what is current monthly traffic and lead volume?
2. Who is the target buyer -- corporates, SMEs, family offices, or brokers?
3. Is there an existing CRM / lead-capture stack we'd be replacing, or starting from zero?
4. Brand authority: is "iqbiz" the long-term brand, or a placeholder?

**Contacts notes:**

- Escrow agent

**Questions:**

2. What is the typical face-value range and historical success rate on closed deals?
3. Are you positioned as a monetizer, provider, or facilitator?
4. How are you handling the standard scam vectors (RWA verification, MT760, escrow)?

**Feasibility:** LOW
**Risk:** HIGH

real and named.

**Contacts notes:**
- Verified refinery / allocation holder
- Named Vopak TSA holder
- KYC / compliance partner
- Escrow bank

**Questions:**
1. Who is the verified seller -- refinery, allocation holder, or trader?
2. Is there a real Vopak tank lease with a named TSA, or are we still at LOI / SCO stage?
3. What jurisdictions and product grades (EN590, JP54, D6)?
4. Any prior closed transactions you can reference?
