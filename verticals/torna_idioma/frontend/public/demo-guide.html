Sign in

Open aiagent.ringlypro.com/Torna_Idioma/login and sign in with the account above.

Cinco Raíces — five words a night

In the left menu open Cinco Raíces. Show today's 5 Spanish words, flip a card to reveal the meaning, and tap Good on a couple. Point to the streak and the words-per-year projection.

AI Spanish Tutor — Profesora Isabel

Open AI Spanish Tutor. Type "Hola, teach me my first word" and send. Press the play button to hear Isabel's voice. Slide Immersion up for more Spanish, then tap Día de Español.

Translation Atelier

Open Translation Atelier. Pick a sample line, type a quick Spanish translation, and press Grade my translation to show the instant feedback.

Rizal Studies + Completion Record

Open Rizal Studies. Open a section (e.g. Noli Me Tángere), show the original Spanish + the simplified version, press Mark section complete, then View Completion Record and Print.

Emperador — leaderboard

Open Emperador to show the leaderboard, the rotating Emperador title, and the badges.

The bilingual flip

Top-left, switch the language toggle EN → FIL to show the whole interface turn to Filipino instantly.

Two quick tips

  • Press play voice once (step 3) before the meeting so the browser allows audio.
  • Start in EN; save the FIL flip (step 7) as your closing "wow."
