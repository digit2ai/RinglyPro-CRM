## AI Voice Assistants

### Rachel (English Voice Assistant)

**What Rachel Does:**
- Answers your business phone 24/7 in English
- Provides professional greeting with your business name
- Captures caller information with names and phone numbers
- Offers options: Book appointment or leave a voicemail
- Automatically transcribes messages and stores them in your Messages tab

**How to Activate:**
1. Go to Dashboard Settings
2. Toggle "Rachel AI" to **ON** (green)
3. Copy your RinglyPro 10-digit phone number
4. Dial your carrier's call forwarding activation code (varies by carrier)
5. Follow your carrier's prompts with your RinglyPro number

**How It Works (Customer Perspective):**
1. Customer calls your business number
2. Rachel answers with greeting: "Thanks for calling [Your Business]. Press 1 for English or 2 for Spanish"
3. Rachel provides main menu: "You can book an appointment or leave a message"
4. Customer chooses appointment or voicemail
5. Rachel collects name, phone number, and message/appointment details

**Deactivate:**
1. Toggle "Rachel AI" to **OFF** (grey)
2. Dial your carrier's call forwarding deactivation code
3. Confirm with your carrier

### Lina (Spanish Voice Assistant)

**What Lina Does:**
- Identical to Rachel but speaks Spanish
- Perfect for bilingual businesses
- Same features: appointments, voicemail, transcription
- Automatically transcribes Spanish audio

**How to Use:**
- Enable Lina in Settings (toggle "Lina AI" to ON)
- Customers can select Spanish (Option 2) when calling
- Messages appear in Messages tab with Spanish audio
- English transcription also provided when available

### Managing Both Rachel and Lina
- You can have both active simultaneously
- Callers choose language at start
- All messages centralized in one Messages tab
- Each has separate activation codes

#### Single Email

Track your email performance:
- **Delivery Rate** - % of emails successfully delivered

## Troubleshooting

### Rachel Not Answering Calls

**Symptoms:** Calls go to voicemail, Rachel doesn't answer

**Quick Fix:**
1. Check Rachel toggle is ON (green) in Settings
2. Verify call forwarding activation code dialed correctly
3. Confirm you dialed your carrier's code (not our code)
4. Test by calling your business number
5. Wait 1-2 minutes for system to sync

**If Still Not Working:**
1. Toggle Rachel OFF
2. Dial deactivation code
3. Wait 30 seconds
4. Toggle Rachel ON
5. Dial activation code again
6. Test again

**Contact Support:**
- Email: info@digit2ai.com
- Include: Your phone number, carrier name, when it stopped working

### Messages Not Appearing

**Symptoms:** Calls go through but no message saved

**Try These:**
1. Refresh page (F5)
2. Check Messages tab
3. Clear browser cache
4. Try different browser
