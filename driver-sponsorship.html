About Óscar Tunjo

Óscar Andrés Tunjo is a Colombian professional racing driver with over 15 years of international motorsport experience. From multiple Karting Championships across Colombia, Latin America, Spain, USA, and Italy to competing at the highest levels of European and American prototype racing.

At the heart of his career are three core values: performance, dedication, and passion. These values are the driving force behind every race, every lap, and every victory achieved with Gebhardt Motorsport.

  • 2025: IMSA VP Winner Mid-Ohio, Norisring Winner, Lap Record Spa-Francorchamps LMP3
  • 2021: GT World Challenge Winner England, 2nd 24 Hours of Spa-Francorchamps
  • 2015-2016: GP3 Series Winner Austria, 2nd Place Barcelona
  • Team: Gebhardt Motorsport
  • Car: LMP3 Prototype
  • Nationality: Colombian

Gebhardt Motorsport: 50 Years of Racing Excellence

A German motorsport legacy built on engineering precision, competitive spirit, and international success.

Origins & History

Gebhardt Motorsport is a German motor racing team and constructor with a distinguished history in sports car and prototype racing, founded by brothers Günther and Fritz Gebhardt in the late 1970s.

The team began in open-wheel racing (Formula Super Vee and Formula 3) before transitioning to sports car endurance racing in 1983. They built and entered their own prototype cars—including the Gebhardt JC02 and C-series prototypes—competing in the Deutsche Rennsport Meisterschaft, Interserie, and World Sportscar Championship with Toyota, BMW, Cosworth, and Audi powerplants.

Modern Era & Recent Success

Gebhardt Motorsport has experienced a powerful resurgence, currently competing in the Prototype Cup Germany series and internationally in the IMSA VP Racing SportsCar Challenge. The team fields modern Duqueine LMP3 prototypes (D08) and Ginetta G61-LT-P3 cars, achieving strong results including team and drivers championships across Europe and North America.
