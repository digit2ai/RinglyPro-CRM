Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about working with us.

What's the typical turnaround time?

It depends on the scope of your project. Photo enhancement typically takes 2-3 days, graphic design 3-5 days, and video content 5-7 days. Rush orders are available for an additional fee.

How many revisions do I get?

Starter packages include 2 revision rounds, Growth includes 3, and Pro includes unlimited revisions. We want you to be 100% satisfied with the final product.

What file formats will I receive?

You'll receive files in all commonly used formats: JPG, PNG, PDF for graphics; MP4, MOV for video; and source files (PSD, AI, AE) upon request. We deliver everything optimized for web and print.

Can I use the assets commercially?

Yes! All deliverables come with full commercial-use licensing. You own the final files and can use them across any platform, marketing channel, or product without restrictions.

How do I submit brand assets?

After you submit your request, we'll send you a secure upload link. You can share logos, brand guidelines, color palettes, fonts, and any reference materials. If you don't have brand assets yet, we can create them for you.

Do you create game UI/HUD and store graphics?

Absolutely! We specialize in video game graphics including UI/HUD elements, character concept art, promotional key art, store banners, and in-game marketing assets. We deliver files in formats optimized for Unity, Unreal Engine, and major app stores. Our team has experience with indie games, mobile games, and Steam releases.
