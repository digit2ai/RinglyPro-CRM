Partnership Agreement

KanchoAI.com × Ronin Brotherhood
Version 1.0 | Effective Date: Date of Signing

Parties

This Partnership Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into by and between:

  1. KanchoAI LLC, operating KanchoAI.com ("KanchoAI"), a technology platform provider
  2. Ronin Brotherhood Federation, operating RoninBrotherhood.com ("RB"), a martial arts distribution channel
  3. Unai [Last Name] ("Unai"), a performance-based profit participant

Section 1 — Roles & Responsibilities

1.1 KanchoAI shall be solely responsible for:

  • Platform technology, hosting, uptime, and maintenance
  • Billing, payment processing, and customer accounts
  • Data security, compliance, and customer support
  • Product development and feature updates

1.2 Ronin Brotherhood shall be solely responsible for:

  • Leveraging federation network to introduce and refer schools to KanchoAI
  • Active promotion of KanchoAI to its 150+ senior masters and 1,000+ black belts
  • Providing formal federation endorsement of KanchoAI as the official platform
  • Participation in onboarding calls for the first cohort of schools

1.3

Unai shall provide supporting services to both parties as mutually agreed, with compensation tied to RB's delivery performance.

Section 2 — Profit Share Distribution

2.1 Tiered Waterfall with Proportional Cost Sharing

All profit share is calculated on Net Profit, defined as gross subscription revenue from Ronin Brotherhood-sourced schools minus platform operating costs, payment processing fees, and applicable taxes.

RB Schools Delivered RB Net Profit Share Unai Profit Share KanchoAI Net Profit Share
1–499 schools30%10%60%
500–999 schools35%15%50%
1,000+ schools40%20%40%

2.2 Unai Funding Formula

Unai's profit share is jointly funded at a fixed 40/60 ratio — 40% contributed by Ronin Brotherhood, 60% by KanchoAI — at every tier. This is embedded within the percentages shown in Section 2.1.

2.3 KanchoAI Direct Revenue

Revenue from schools acquired by KanchoAI through its own marketing, advertising, SEO, or independent outreach carries zero profit share obligation to RB or Unai. KanchoAI's independently acquired revenue is 100% retained by KanchoAI.

Section 3 — Attribution Rules

3.1 Full RB Attribution

A school is fully attributed to Ronin Brotherhood when it signs up using RB's unique referral code or link. Full profit share per Section 2.1 applies.

3.2 Full KanchoAI Direct

A school with no Ronin Brotherhood federation affiliation that signs up without a referral code is 100% KanchoAI direct. No profit share applies.

3.3 Split Attribution (Grey Zone)

A verified Ronin Brotherhood federation member who signs up through KanchoAI's own marketing without using the referral code shall be subject to a 50/50 split attribution. RB receives 50% of its applicable tier profit share on that school; KanchoAI retains the remaining 50%.

3.4 Tracking

Attribution is tracked via a unique referral code or link assigned exclusively to Ronin Brotherhood. The referral code is the single source of truth. KanchoAI shall maintain CRM records of all attribution designations.

Section 4 — Exclusivity

4.1 Unconditional Baseline Commitment

KanchoAI shall never directly recruit, solicit, or pursue any member, school, or contact within the Ronin Brotherhood federation at any time during or after this partnership. This commitment is permanent and not subject to any tier threshold.

4.2 Performance-Based External Market Exclusivity

RB Schools DeliveredKanchoAI External Market Exclusivity
1–499 schoolsKanchoAI free to sign any other federation
500–999 schoolsKanchoAI will not sign any new federation partnerships nationally
1,000+ schoolsKanchoAI exclusively partners with Ronin Brotherhood as its sole federation channel

4.3 Exclusivity Maintenance

Exclusivity at each tier is maintained only while the minimum verified active subscription count is sustained. If RB's active school count drops below a tier threshold for 3 consecutive months, exclusivity automatically adjusts to the lower tier.

Section 5 — Payment Schedule

5.1 Frequency

Monthly, covering all verified active subscriptions from the prior calendar month.

5.2 Method

PayPal or Stripe transfer to each party's registered account on file.

5.3 Timing

Net 15. Payment is issued within 15 days after the calendar month closes.

5.4 Monthly Earnings Statement

KanchoAI shall issue a monthly earnings statement to RB and Unai no later than 5 business days after month close. The statement shall include:

  • Total RB-attributed schools active that month
  • Grey zone schools and 50/50 split applied
  • Gross revenue, itemized deductible costs, taxes
  • Net profit and each party's calculated share

5.5 Payment Release

Payment releases upon written acknowledgment (email or platform confirmation) from both RB and Unai of the monthly earnings statement.

5.6 Example Timeline

March 31 — Period closes

April 5 — Earnings statement issued

April 15 — Payment released after acknowledgment

Section 6 — Contract Duration & Renewal

6.1 Term

This Agreement is open-ended, effective from the date of signing, with no fixed expiration date.

6.2 Renewal

The Agreement automatically continues on a month-to-month basis unless either party provides written notice of termination.

6.3 Notice Period

Either party may terminate this Agreement with 30 days written notice delivered via email to all parties.

6.4 Annual Performance Review

Every 12 months from the effective date, all parties shall conduct a joint review to assess school delivery, attribution accuracy, payment compliance, and mutual terms adjustment if agreed in writing.

6.5 Tier Downgrade

If RB's active school count drops below a tier threshold for 3 consecutive months, profit share automatically adjusts to the lower tier without requiring amendment to this Agreement.

Section 7 — Exit Conditions

7.1 Standard Termination

Either party may exit with 30 days written notice, no cause required.

7.2 Immediate Termination by KanchoAI

KanchoAI may terminate this Agreement immediately and without notice if Ronin Brotherhood enters a formal partnership, distribution agreement, or endorsement deal with a competing martial arts school management platform.

7.3 Post-Termination Profit Share Tail

Upon termination, RB and Unai shall continue receiving their respective profit share on already-onboarded active schools for 6 months following the termination date, paid monthly per the Net 15 schedule.

7.4 KanchoAI Retained Rights

Upon exit, KanchoAI retains all school data, platform accounts, billing relationships, technology, and the right to continue serving any school that chooses to remain.

7.5 RB Retained Rights

Upon exit, RB retains its federation network, member relationships, all earned profit share through the 6-month tail period, and the right to endorse any platform after the tail period ends.

7.6 School Migration

The disposition of RB-sourced schools at time of exit shall be resolved by mutual written agreement between KanchoAI and RB.

Section 8 — Government & Law Enforcement Vertical (Addendum A)

8.1 Roles

KanchoAI provides all technology, platform, proposals, compliance documentation, and contract fulfillment. Ronin Brotherhood leverages government and law enforcement contacts, actively pursues and closes deals, and provides introductions and endorsements.

8.2 Revenue Share

RB receives 40% of gross contract value on every government or law enforcement contract they close. KanchoAI retains 60% of gross contract value. This is a gross split — no cost deductions before calculating shares — given the higher contract values and longer sales cycles.

8.3 Payment Timing

RB revenue share on government contracts is paid within 15 days of KanchoAI receiving each government payment installment. For multi-installment contracts, RB receives their 40% proportionally with each installment.

8.4 Attribution

A government contract is attributed to RB only if they made the initial introduction or were actively involved in closing the deal, documented in writing at the time of introduction.

8.5 Scope

Covers law enforcement agencies, military, municipal governments, and federal training programs. Any other government vertical requires mutual written agreement before RB attribution applies.

Section 9 — Dispute Resolution

9.1 Internal Resolution

Before any formal action, all parties must attempt to resolve the dispute directly through good faith negotiation for a minimum of 30 days from the date the dispute is raised in writing.

9.2 Mediation

If unresolved after 30 days, all parties agree to engage a neutral third-party mediator. Mediation costs are split equally between KanchoAI and RB.

9.3 Governing Law

This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Florida.

9.4 Jurisdiction

Any formal proceedings shall take place in the courts of the State of Florida.

9.5 Business Continuity

During any dispute or mediation, all parties agree to continue fulfilling their obligations. Profit share payments continue on schedule, schools continue to be serviced, and neither party may use a dispute as grounds to withhold payment or terminate without proper notice.

Section 10 — General Provisions

10.1 Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior discussions, negotiations, and understandings.

10.2 Amendments

Any amendment to this Agreement must be made in writing and signed by all parties.

10.3 Severability

If any provision of this Agreement is found unenforceable, the remaining provisions remain in full force.

10.4 Confidentiality

All parties agree to keep the terms of this Agreement confidential and not disclose them to third parties without written consent.
