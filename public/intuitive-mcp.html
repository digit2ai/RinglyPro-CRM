From Horizontal Network → Monolithic Brain

Today the six divisions are parallel rails. With MCP they become one cortex.

Intuitive’s six divisions are world-class on their own, but they were never engineered to think together. A Clinical Sales Rep closing a hospital deal can’t see what the proctoring schedule looks like 30 days out. A Field Service Engineer logging a fault has no automatic line to R&D’s defect tracker. Finance posts revenue without knowing the surgeon’s ramp curve. Every handoff is a meeting, a spreadsheet, or a Slack ping. The MCP brain removes the handoff.

Why SurgicalMind Exists

SurgicalMind is the first MCP brain built for Intuitive’s field organization.

SurgicalMind was created to prove a specific thesis: a single MCP-native layer can compress Intuitive’s longest workflow — from surgeon interest to hospital go-live to sustained da Vinci utilization — from 18 months of fragmented activity into a continuously-orchestrated, evidence-driven operating loop. It is the operator brain that sits on top of the six divisions and gives every Area Sales Manager, Clinical Sales Rep, and hospital executive the same source of truth.

Problem 1

Surgeon Survey

Capture surgeon clinical signal in 6 minutes — volumes, OR access, blockers, interest in da Vinci — with a magic-link survey that auto-routes to the right CSR.

Problem 2

Clinical Dollarization

Convert surgeon-reported case mix into a defensible $ figure for the hospital CFO: incremental revenue, displaced cost, OR throughput, length-of-stay impact.

Problem 3

Proforma Tracker

Track committed cases vs. actuals month-over-month. Net-new (Splitter) and converted volumes scored separately so claims stay honest and finance stays calm.

Problem 4

Business Plan Generator

One click produces the hospital business plan deck — surgeon roster, dollarization, proforma, ROI, training plan — ready to walk into the C-suite meeting.
