01 -- How It Works

Upload to Diagnosis in Seconds

No buttons to click. No waiting. The AI reads every image automatically the moment it is uploaded.

1

Patient Uploads Image

Drag and drop an X-ray, CT scan, or MRI into the case. Supports JPEG, PNG, DICOM up to 500MB.

2

AI Analyzes Instantly

Within 10-30 seconds, the AI identifies the modality, body region, and every visible finding. Clinical context from the case is used to improve accuracy.

3

Structured Report Generated

Findings, impression, abnormalities, ICD-10 codes, follow-up recommendations, and confidence level -- all in a structured format.

4

Radiologist Reviews and Finalizes

The AI draft feeds into the diagnostic copilot. The doctor reviews, edits, and signs off in 5 minutes instead of 30.

5

Patient Gets Answers

Finalized report available immediately. Patient can export as PDF, talk to Rachel for a plain-language explanation, or discuss in their next appointment.

05 -- Technical Architecture

Designed for Reliability and Speed

Non-Blocking Upload

The upload returns immediately. AI analysis runs in the background. By the time you scroll down, the analysis is usually already complete.

Persistent Database Storage

Images are stored in the PostgreSQL database, not on temporary filesystems. Images survive server restarts and infrastructure changes.

Clinical Context Injection

Before analysis, the system gathers the patient's clinical data. A knee image from a trauma case is interpreted differently than one from a degenerative case.

Structured JSON Output

The AI returns a defined schema that feeds into the copilot, Rachel voice assistant, PDF exports, and the case timeline -- not unstructured free text.
