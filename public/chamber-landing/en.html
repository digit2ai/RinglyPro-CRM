How It Works: The Member Journey

From signup to your first business match in under 5 minutes.

1

Sign Up & Build Your Profile

Register with your name, sector, and region. Your profile joins the directory -- visible to other members, invisible to outsiders.

2

Explore the Dashboard

Sign in to your dashboard. View network statistics, browse the directory, and check your trust score.

3

Run Your First AI Match

Type what you're looking for ("food partner in Mexico") and get ranked results with similarity and confidence scores.

4

Post or Respond to a Quote

Need a vendor? Post a request on the Trade Exchange. Have capacity? Browse RFQs and submit proposals.

5

Propose or Join a Project

Have an idea? Propose it. The platform assembles a multi-region team and simulates viability with Monte Carlo.

6

Watch the Network Grow

Every interaction builds trust, every project lifts scores. The HCI index tracks it all -- you can see the impact you generate.
