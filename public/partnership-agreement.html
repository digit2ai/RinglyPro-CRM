1. Introduction and Purpose

This Partnership Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into between RinglyPro ("Provider") and the undersigned individual or entity ("Partner") for the purpose of establishing a mutually beneficial revenue-sharing partnership. The Partner agrees to promote, market, and refer customers to RinglyPro's AI-powered CRM services in exchange for a share of the profits generated from those referred customers.

2. Partnership Structure and Revenue Share

2.1 Revenue Share Model

50% Profit Share

Partner shall receive fifty percent (50%) of the net profits generated from all customers directly referred by Partner and verified through Partner's unique referral link or code.

2.2 Definition of Net Profits

"Net Profits" are defined as gross revenue received from referred customers minus:

  • Direct cost of services (AI API costs, telephony costs, infrastructure)
  • Payment processing fees (Stripe, PayPal, etc.)
  • Refunds and chargebacks
  • Sales tax and applicable regulatory fees

2.3 Lifetime Revenue

Partner shall continue to receive their revenue share for the entire lifetime of each referred customer's active subscription, including all renewals, upgrades, and add-on services purchased by that customer.

3. Partner Obligations and Responsibilities

3.1 Marketing and Promotion

Partner agrees to:

  • Actively promote RinglyPro services through ethical marketing practices
  • Use only approved marketing materials provided by RinglyPro
  • Accurately represent RinglyPro's features, pricing, and capabilities
  • Comply with all applicable advertising laws and regulations (FTC, CAN-SPAM, GDPR, etc.)
  • Not engage in spam, false advertising, or misleading claims

3.2 Brand Compliance

Partner shall not modify RinglyPro's branding, logos, or trademarks without written permission. All promotional materials must be approved by Provider before public use.

3.3 Prohibited Activities

Partner shall NOT:

  • Engage in cookie stuffing, click fraud, or other fraudulent referral methods
  • Self-refer or create fake accounts to generate commissions
  • Bid on RinglyPro's brand name in paid search campaigns
  • Use deceptive marketing tactics or make false claims
  • Violate any laws, regulations, or ethical marketing standards

4. Provider Obligations

4.1 Services and Support

Provider agrees to:

  • Provide Partner with a unique referral tracking link/code
  • Deliver high-quality CRM services to all referred customers
  • Maintain accurate tracking and reporting of referred customers
  • Provide Partner with access to a dashboard showing referral performance
  • Offer marketing materials and resources to support Partner's promotional efforts
  • Handle all customer service, billing, and technical support

4.2 Payment Terms

Monthly Payouts

  • Payment Schedule: Monthly, within 15 days of month-end
  • Minimum Payout: $100 USD (earnings below minimum carry over to next month)
  • Payment Methods: Bank transfer (ACH/Wire), PayPal, or Stripe
  • Reporting: Detailed commission statement provided with each payment

5. Term and Termination

5.1 Initial Term

This Agreement begins upon signature and continues indefinitely until terminated by either party.

5.2 Termination Rights

Either party may terminate this Agreement with:

  • 30 days written notice for termination without cause
  • Immediate termination for material breach of this Agreement
  • Immediate termination for fraudulent activity or violation of prohibited activities

5.3 Post-Termination

Important: Lifetime Commissions Protected

Upon termination, Partner shall continue to receive revenue share from all customers referred prior to the termination date for the lifetime of those customers' subscriptions, provided the termination was not due to Partner's breach or fraudulent activity.

6. Intellectual Property

All RinglyPro trademarks, logos, brand materials, and intellectual property remain the exclusive property of Provider. Partner is granted a limited, non-exclusive, revocable license to use RinglyPro's branding solely for the purpose of promoting the partnership during the term of this Agreement.

7. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to keep confidential all proprietary information, customer data, commission structures, and business strategies disclosed during this partnership. This obligation survives termination of this Agreement.

8. Representations and Warranties

8.1 Partner Represents:

  • Partner has the legal authority to enter into this Agreement
  • Partner will comply with all applicable laws and regulations
  • Partner owns or has rights to use any promotional materials they create
  • Partner's marketing activities will not infringe on third-party rights

8.2 Provider Represents:

  • Provider has the authority to offer this partnership program
  • RinglyPro services will be provided in a professional manner
  • Provider will accurately track and report referrals
  • Provider will pay commissions as specified in this Agreement

9. Limitation of Liability

Neither party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, special, consequential, or punitive damages. Provider's total liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the total commissions paid to Partner in the twelve (12) months preceding the claim.

10. Independent Contractor Relationship

Partner is an independent contractor, not an employee, agent, or joint venturer of Provider. Partner is responsible for all taxes, insurance, and expenses related to their business activities.

11. Dispute Resolution

Any disputes arising from this Agreement shall first be attempted to be resolved through good-faith negotiation. If unresolved within 30 days, disputes may be submitted to binding arbitration in accordance with the rules of the American Arbitration Association.

12. General Provisions

12.1 Entire Agreement

This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior agreements, understandings, and communications.

12.2 Amendments

Provider may modify the terms of this Agreement with 30 days written notice. Partner's continued participation after notice constitutes acceptance of the modified terms. Material changes to commission structure require Partner's written consent.

12.3 Assignment

Partner may not assign this Agreement without Provider's written consent. Provider may assign this Agreement in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of business.

12.4 Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of [State], without regard to conflict of law principles.

12.5 Severability

If any provision of this Agreement is found to be unenforceable, the remaining provisions shall remain in full effect.
