Delete Your Account

Request deletion of your RinglyPro account and associated data

We respect your right to control your personal data. If you wish to delete your RinglyPro account and all associated data, please follow the steps below.

How to Request Account Deletion

1

Send an Email Request

Email us at info@ringlypro.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request"

2

Include Your Account Information

Provide the email address associated with your RinglyPro account and your business name for verification

3

Confirmation & Processing

We will verify your identity and process your request within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once complete.

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Account Information: Name, email, phone number, and password
  • Business Data: Business name, description, and settings
  • CRM Data: Contacts, appointments, and call logs stored in your account
  • Usage Data: Token balance, credit history, and call recordings
  • Integrations: Any connected third-party services (GoHighLevel, etc.)

Data Retention Notice

Some data may be retained for a limited period as required by law:

  • Transaction Records: Billing and payment history may be retained for up to 7 years for tax and legal compliance
  • Communication Logs: Certain call metadata may be retained for regulatory compliance (A2P 10DLC, Twilio requirements)
