What Is Neural Intelligence?

Neural Intelligence is an AI reasoning layer that sits at the center of your business operations. Instead of bolting another dashboard onto your stack, it connects every relevant system you already use — CRM, phones, financials, inventory, compliance, vendor data — through the Model Context Protocol (MCP), and then reasons across all of them simultaneously to surface what matters and prescribe what to do next.

Most AI tools tell you what happened. Neural Intelligence tells you what to do about it — with the context of every connected system, in real time, 24/7. AI recommends. Humans decide.

Multi-Source Reasoning

One AI brain analyzes your CRM, phones, ledgers, sanctions lists, ops data, and customer history — together, not in silos.

Pattern Recognition

Detects fraud, churn risk, downtime, compliance gaps, and growth signals that rigid rule-based systems can never catch.

Diagnostic + Prescriptive

Every finding ships with a fix. "Here's what is wrong, here's what to do, here's the workflow that does it."

Adaptive Learning

Continuously refines its judgment from your team's feedback — gets smarter every week without re-engineering.
