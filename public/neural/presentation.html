What It Is

RinglyPro Neural is a free, instant diagnostic tool. A prospect uploads a contact or call export from their CRM, and the page scores their sales operation, lists every problem it can find, and offers a one-click fix for each one.

  • A diagnostic, not a database. Nothing is stored on our servers. The whole analysis happens in the visitor's browser.
  • Works with the four CRMs that matter. GoHighLevel, HubSpot, Zoho, and RinglyPro itself.
  • Built for sales conversations. The output is a screen the prospect wants to talk about — that's the demo opener.

How It Works — In Plain English

Seven steps, all happening live in the prospect's browser.

1

The prospect drops a file

They export contacts or calls from their CRM as a CSV — every CRM has this — and drag it onto our page.

Or they click one of our four sample files and skip the export step entirely.
2

We read the file

The browser opens the CSV and reads its column names and rows. Nothing is uploaded anywhere.

3

We figure out which CRM it came from

Each CRM has its own column-name fingerprint. We look at the headers and say "this is HubSpot" — the green banner that pops up confirming the match is what builds trust.

4

We translate everything to a common language

Different CRMs use different field names — "First Name" vs "firstName" vs "FIRSTNAME." We rename everything to one shared format so the rest of the analysis doesn't care where the data came from.

5

Ten checks scan the data

Each check looks for one specific problem — missed calls, slow lead response, no-shows, stale pipeline, after-hours coverage gaps, and so on. Each one that finds an issue creates a "Finding."

These are smart, hand-tuned business rules — not a black box. Reproducible and explainable.
6

We score the operation

Start at 100, subtract points for each finding (more for critical ones, less for medium ones). The result is the big health score in the ring.

7

We show findings + fixes side-by-side

Every finding has an "Activate Fix" button. Clicking it launches a real automation workflow on our backend — that is the moment the prospect becomes a paying customer.

Anticipated Questions

Be ready for these:

Is the "Neural" name AI?

The findings come from carefully tuned business rules, not a generative model. We chose rules over AI on purpose: rules are reproducible, explainable, and cannot hallucinate a problem the data doesn't actually show. The "Neural" branding refers to the network of checks running in parallel.

Where does the data go?

Nowhere. The CSV is read inside the browser tab and discarded when the tab closes. No server upload, no logging, no storage.

What happens when I click "Activate Fix"?

That is where Neural connects to the RinglyPro platform. Activating a fix triggers a real automation workflow inside the customer's account — for example, an auto-callback for missed calls or a reminder sequence for stale leads.

What if my CRM isn't one of the four supported?

Most CRMs can export in a CSV format close to one of the supported four — usually HubSpot's. We can also add a custom mapper in a few hours.

How is the health score calculated?

The score starts at 100 and loses points for each finding. Critical findings cost more, medium findings cost less. The exact weights are tuned so the score lines up with what an experienced sales operator would say after looking at the same data.
