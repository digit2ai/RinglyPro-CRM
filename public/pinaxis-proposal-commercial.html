Digit2AI proposes to license the PINAXIS Warehouse Analytics Platform to Pinaxis under a white-label annual license. Pinaxis will operate the platform under their own brand and resell warehouse analytics services to end customers worldwide.

The platform is production-ready today, with 12 analysis modules, AI-powered proposals, voice-narrated presentations, OEE tracking, and a full REST API -- all built and tested over the past 7 weeks.

This Platform License Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of the Effective Date by and between:

ProviderDigit2AI LLC, a Florida limited liability company ("Digit2AI")
LicenseePinaxis, a company organized under the laws of Germany ("Pinaxis")

1. Definitions

  • "Platform" means the PINAXIS Warehouse Analytics software, including all source code, container images, databases, APIs, dashboards, analysis modules, and documentation developed by Digit2AI.
  • "White-Label Rights" means the right to rebrand, customize, and operate the Platform under Pinaxis's own brand identity.
  • "End Customer" means any third party to whom Pinaxis provides access to or services based on the Platform.
  • "End-Customer Revenue" means all fees, subscriptions, setup charges, per-project fees, and support revenue collected by Pinaxis from End Customers in connection with the Platform.
  • "License Term" means the initial 12-month period from the Effective Date, and each subsequent 12-month renewal period.

2. Grant of License

2.1. License. Digit2AI grants Pinaxis a non-exclusive, non-transferable, worldwide license to use, operate, modify (for branding purposes), and sublicense the Platform to End Customers during the License Term.

2.2. White-Label. Pinaxis may operate the Platform under its own brand, remove Digit2AI attribution, and customize visual elements. Pinaxis may not represent itself as the developer of the Platform's core technology.

2.3. Deployment. Pinaxis may deploy the Platform on its own infrastructure (on-premises), cloud, or hybrid environments. Digit2AI will assist with the initial deployment as described in Section 5.

3. Fees & Payment

3.1. Annual License Fee. Pinaxis shall pay Digit2AI an annual license fee of $140,000 USD per License Term ("License Fee").

3.2. Good-Faith Deposit. Upon execution of this Agreement, Pinaxis shall pay a good-faith deposit of $15,000 USD, which shall be credited against the first License Fee. The deposit is non-refundable.

3.3. License Balance. The remaining $125,000 USD of the first License Fee is due Net-30 from platform go-live on Pinaxis infrastructure ("Go-Live Date").

3.4. Revenue Share. Pinaxis shall pay Digit2AI fifty percent (50%) of all End-Customer Revenue. Revenue share payments are due monthly, Net-30, accompanied by a revenue report detailing each End Customer, fees collected, and applicable period.

3.5. Renewal. Subsequent License Terms renew automatically at $140,000 USD per year unless either party provides 90-day written notice of non-renewal.

3.6. Late Payment. Overdue amounts bear interest at 1.5% per month or the maximum rate permitted by law, whichever is less.

4. Intellectual Property

4.1. Ownership. Digit2AI retains all right, title, and interest in and to the Platform, including all source code, algorithms, architectures, databases, and documentation. This Agreement does not transfer any IP ownership to Pinaxis.

4.2. Pinaxis Customizations. Any branding, visual modifications, or configurations made by Pinaxis are owned by Pinaxis. Any modifications to core Platform functionality require Digit2AI's written approval.

4.3. End-Customer Data. All data uploaded or generated by End Customers is owned by the respective End Customer. Neither Digit2AI nor Pinaxis may use End-Customer data for purposes outside of providing the Platform services.

5. Deployment & Support

5.1. Initial Deployment. Digit2AI will deploy the Platform on Pinaxis's designated infrastructure within 6-8 weeks of receiving infrastructure access. Deployment includes schema migration, configuration, SSO integration, and admin training.

5.2. On-Premises Installation. Scope and pricing for on-premises installation services are subject to mutual agreement and may be included within or in addition to the License Fee, as agreed by the parties.

5.3. Updates. Digit2AI will deliver quarterly feature releases and security patches via private container registry or offline delivery. Digit2AI will provide database migration scripts and release notes for each update.

5.4. Support Tiers. L3 engineering support (bug fixes, escalation, architecture guidance) is included in the License Fee. L1/L2 end-customer support is Pinaxis's responsibility.

6. Revenue Reporting & Audit

6.1. Monthly Reports. Pinaxis shall provide Digit2AI with a monthly revenue report within 15 days of each month-end, detailing End-Customer names, fees collected, and services rendered.

6.2. Audit Rights. Digit2AI may, upon 30 days' written notice, audit Pinaxis's books and records related to End-Customer Revenue, no more than once per calendar year. If an audit reveals underpayment exceeding 5%, Pinaxis shall bear the cost of the audit.

6.3. Shared Dashboard. The parties agree to maintain a shared revenue tracking dashboard for real-time transparency, to be implemented within 90 days of Go-Live.

7. Confidentiality

7.1. Each party agrees to maintain the confidentiality of the other party's proprietary information, including but not limited to source code, pricing, customer lists, and business strategies.

7.2. This obligation survives termination for a period of 3 years.

8. Non-Compete

8.1. During the License Term and for 12 months following termination, Pinaxis shall not develop, commission, or acquire a competing warehouse data analytics platform that replicates the core functionality of the Platform.

8.2. This clause does not restrict Pinaxis from using third-party analytics tools that do not directly compete with the Platform's core warehouse analysis capabilities.

9. Term & Termination

9.1. Term. This Agreement begins on the Effective Date and continues for successive 12-month License Terms unless terminated.

9.2. Termination for Convenience. Either party may terminate by providing 90 days' written notice before the end of the current License Term.

9.3. Termination for Cause. Either party may terminate immediately upon written notice if the other party materially breaches and fails to cure within 30 days of written notice.

9.4. Effect of Termination. Upon termination: (a) Pinaxis's license to the Platform ceases; (b) Pinaxis shall wind down End-Customer access within 90 days; (c) all outstanding revenue share payments become immediately due; (d) Pinaxis shall return or destroy all Platform materials.

9.5. Survival. Sections 4, 6, 7, 8, and 10 survive termination.

10. Limitation of Liability

10.1. Neither party shall be liable for indirect, incidental, consequential, or punitive damages.

10.2. Digit2AI's total aggregate liability under this Agreement shall not exceed the License Fees paid by Pinaxis in the 12 months preceding the claim.

11. General Provisions

11.1. Governing Law. This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Florida, USA.

11.2. Dispute Resolution. Any disputes shall be resolved by binding arbitration in Miami, Florida, under the rules of the American Arbitration Association.

11.3. Entire Agreement. This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior discussions, proposals, and agreements.

11.4. Amendments. Amendments must be in writing and signed by both parties.

11.5. Severability. If any provision is found unenforceable, the remaining provisions continue in full force.

11.6. Assignment. Neither party may assign this Agreement without the other party's written consent, except in connection with a merger or acquisition.

12. Source Code Escrow

12.1. Upon Pinaxis's request, Digit2AI shall deposit the Platform source code with a mutually agreed-upon escrow agent. Release conditions include: (a) Digit2AI ceases business operations; (b) Digit2AI materially breaches and fails to cure.
