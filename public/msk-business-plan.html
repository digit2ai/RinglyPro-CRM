02 — The Opportunity

A $9.4B Market with Zero Dominant Player

Musculoskeletal conditions are the #1 cause of disability globally. 1.71 billion people are affected. Remote specialist access barely exists.

The Gap

70% of orthopedic specialists concentrate in urban centers. Rural and island communities — home to 3.4 billion people globally — have near-zero MSK specialist access. Patients travel hours, pay hundreds, and wait weeks.

The Timing

Three converging forces: Starlink bringing broadband to every corner of Earth, AI capable of clinical-grade assessment, and post-COVID acceptance of telehealth. This combination was impossible 3 years ago.

The Moat

No competitor combines AI voice intake + camera ROM measurement + AI report generation + satellite readiness in a single browser app. Our platform is live today. Competitors are still building slide decks.

04 — Go-to-Market Strategy

Philippines First, Then the World

1

Phase 1: Philippines Pilot (Month 1-6)

Deploy in 1 province (Eastern Samar). 5 barangay health stations + 1 mobile medical van. Partner with provincial health office. Target: 1,600 patients/month. Validate unit economics and clinical outcomes.

2

Phase 2: Philippines Scale (Month 7-12)

Expand to 3 provinces. Deploy 15 stations + 3 vans. Apply for PhilHealth integration. Publish clinical outcomes data. Target: 6,600 patients/month. Begin Tagalog voice AI localization.

3

Phase 3: National + First Expansion (Month 13-24)

20 provinces in Philippines (33,000 patients/month). Launch in Indonesia (250M population, similar island geography). Explore India rural health partnerships. White-label licensing to hospital networks.

4

Phase 4: Global Platform (Month 25-36)

Active in 5+ countries. Government telehealth contracts. WHO/USAID partnership. Sports medicine vertical (professional teams). Workers' compensation vertical. Target: 200,000+ patients/month across all markets.

Target Customer Segments

B2G — Governments

National telehealth programs, provincial health offices, universal health coverage initiatives. Revenue: deployment contracts + per-patient fees.

B2B — Organizations

Sports teams, employers (workers' comp), hospital networks, insurance companies, NGOs. Revenue: subscription + per-case fees.

B2C — Patients

Athletes, active individuals, anyone with MSK pain seeking fast specialist access. Revenue: per-case ($299-$2,999) + subscription.

Why $3M Is an Early-Mover Advantage

Independent technical assessment values this platform at $4M–$5M pre-money today. By investing at a $3M valuation, seed investors are entering at a significant discount to fair value. Here is why:

Not a Prototype

12,452 lines of production code. 37 database tables. 110 API endpoints. 24 frontend pages. This is a full clinical-grade platform live on the internet right now. Most seed-stage companies have a landing page and a pitch deck.

Regulatory Features Done

FHIR R4 compliance, Remote Patient Monitoring with CPT billing, insurance claims engine, and a Workers' Compensation module. These are hardcore regulatory features that take months to build. Any hospital or insurance partner will require them. They are done.

AI Copilot Is Live

Not planned. Not mocked. The AI diagnostic copilot connects to Claude, ingests all case data — ROM measurements, PROMs, triage, imaging, messages — and generates a full radiology report draft. A radiologist reviews and finalizes in 5 minutes instead of 30. This is the core differentiator and it is working today.

Patentable Technology

Camera-based Range of Motion assessment measures joint angles from a phone camera using Google MediaPipe AI. No special hardware needed. Replaces a $50+ physical goniometer and a trained clinician. Works offline. This feature alone has patent potential.

Replacement cost: $500,000+ — and that is just the code. It does not include the domain knowledge, clinical protocol design, architecture decisions, and months of debugging that went into building a system this comprehensive. At a $3M pre-money valuation, seed investors are acquiring equity at a 40–60% discount to the platform's assessed value. This pricing will not be available after the seed round closes.
