What is PACC-CFL?

Clarity about our mission and model for Filipino-American business empowerment in Florida.

Founded in 1995, the Philippine American Chamber of Commerce - Central Florida (PACC-CFL) is a vibrant hub connecting local, regional, and overseas Filipino-American businesses. Our mission is to empower entrepreneurs and businesses by fostering a collaborative environment for growth and success.

For over 30 years, PACC-CFL has served as a bridge between the Filipino-American business community in Central Florida and opportunities across the United States, the Philippines, and the ASEAN region. We are not a social club -- we are an action-driven business network that creates real connections, facilitates trade, and champions the interests of Filipino-American professionals and entrepreneurs.

6 Strategic Pillars

The foundations driving Filipino-American business growth in Central Florida and beyond.

Business Networking

Expanded network of business leaders and entrepreneurs. Connecting Filipino-American professionals across industries through curated events, B2B introductions, and our AI-powered matching platform.

Membership Growth

Connecting professionals across Central Florida. Strategic outreach to healthcare workers, tech professionals, entrepreneurs, and community leaders throughout all five Florida regions.

Trade & Commerce

International trade connectivity through ASEAN-Florida corridors. Facilitating bilateral trade between Central Florida businesses and Philippine markets, with focus on emerging sectors and supply chains.

Education & Development

Business Mastermind Series, workshops, and professional development programs. Equipping members with skills in AI, cybersecurity, digital marketing, and business strategy.

Advocacy & Representation

Supporting Filipino-American business interests at local, state, and federal levels. Policy advocacy, regulatory engagement, and ensuring our community's voice is heard in economic development decisions.

Cultural Exchange

Celebrating 461 years of Manila Galleon trade heritage and the Mexico-Philippines partnership. Bridging cultures through business, fostering understanding, and strengthening the Filipino-American identity in Florida.

Priority Sectors

Key industries where Filipino-American professionals and businesses lead in Central Florida.

Healthcare & Professional Services

The largest Filipino-American workforce sectors in Florida. Nurses, physicians, dentists, accountants, and professionals driving excellence across Central Florida's healthcare and services industries.

Technology & Cybersecurity

AI, automation, and digital transformation workshops. Supporting Filipino-American tech professionals and entrepreneurs building innovative solutions in Florida's growing tech corridor.

Food Service & Hospitality

Restaurant, catering, and hospitality businesses that bring Filipino cuisine and warmth to Florida. From lumpia to lechon, our members are shaping the state's vibrant food landscape.

Real Estate & Construction

Growing Filipino-American presence in Florida's property market. Real estate agents, developers, contractors, and investors building communities and creating wealth across Central Florida.

Programs & Events

Signature initiatives that bring our community together and drive business growth.

Business Mastermind Series

Monthly deep-dive sessions with successful Filipino-American entrepreneurs. Strategy, scaling, and real-world business lessons from leaders who've built it.

East Meets West Global Business Conference

Annual conference bridging US and Philippine business ecosystems. Keynotes, panels, B2B matchmaking, and trade facilitation in one powerful event.

Mexico-Philippines Friendship Festival

Celebrating the 461st anniversary of the Manila Galleon Trade route. Cultural exchange, culinary fusion, and strengthening the historic Mexico-Philippines partnership.

AI & Technology Workshops

Hands-on training in artificial intelligence, automation, and digital tools. Helping members leverage technology to grow their businesses and careers.

Cybersecurity Training

Protecting Filipino-American businesses from digital threats. Practical cybersecurity education for business owners, from data protection to compliance best practices.

Networking Mixers & Gala Events

Quarterly networking events and annual gala celebrating Filipino-American business achievement. Build relationships in a warm, professional environment.
