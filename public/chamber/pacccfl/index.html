The AI Engine Under the Hood

7 mathematical models working behind every interaction on the platform.

1

Cosine Similarity

Matches member profiles as 9-dimensional vectors. Sector, region, experience, languages, trust -- all weighted and compared mathematically.

2

Gini Fairness

Measures regional inequality in opportunity distribution. Boosts underserved regions so Tampa members get the same exposure as Orlando.

3

TrustRank

PageRank-style algorithm that propagates trust through the network. Verified, active, project-completing members score higher.

4

Monte Carlo

10,000-iteration simulation for project viability. Triangular distributions on cost and timeline produce probability scores before you invest.

5

HCI Index

5-pillar composite: equity + trust + network value + project success + activation rate. One number that tells you if your chamber is healthy.
