The AI Engine Under the Hood

8 AI models and agents working behind every interaction.

1

Compliance Screening

Automatic verification against OFAC, EU, UN and PEP lists. Multilingual fuzzy matching with 90% reduction in false positives.

2

Cosine Similarity

Compares profiles as 9-dimensional vectors. Sector, region, experience, languages -- all mathematically weighted.

3

Gini Fairness

Measures regional inequality. Boosts under-served regions so everyone gets equal exposure.

4

TrustRank

PageRank-style algorithm that propagates trust. Verified, active members get higher scores.

5

Monte Carlo

10,000 iterations for project viability. Triangular distributions produce probabilities before investing.
