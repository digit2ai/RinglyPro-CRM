Trade Development

Filipino MSMEs need not just a local market, but a bridge to ASEAN and beyond. PCCI builds that bridge — sector by sector, corridor by corridor.

ASEAN trade corridors connecting Philippine exporters to Vietnam, Thailand, Indonesia, and Singapore through pre-vetted partnerships and compliance guidance.

Export-readiness programs that certify MSMEs for international trade, with documentation templates and regulatory navigation.

Bilateral agreements facilitated between Philippine chambers and their counterparts across 12 priority sectors.

Capital follows trust. Trust follows transparency. PCCI makes both programmable — from PEZA zones to Stripe-integrated escrow.

PEZA Economic Zones — direct pathways to incentivized zones for manufacturing, IT-BPO, and agri-processing ventures.

Cross-Border Investment facilitated through escrow-backed project funding. Stripe-integrated membership payments ensure seamless transactions globally.
