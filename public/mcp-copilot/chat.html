Tips

Be Natural: You can combine info in one message
create contact john 555-1234 john@test.com
Confirmation: The assistant will always confirm before executing actions. Just reply yes or confirm to proceed.
Flexible Format: Commands work in various formats
find john = search for john = show me john
Phone Numbers: Any format works - 555-1234, (555) 123-4567, 5551234567

Common Workflow: New Customer Onboarding

1. create contact Sarah Smith 555-1234 sarah@email.com 2. add tag new customer to sarah 3. send email to sarah subject Welcome body Thanks for choosing us! 4. create opportunity for sarah $5000 in sales pipeline
