The Digital Ecosystem

CamaraVirtual: the platform that turns a network of contacts into real business.

PACC-CFL runs on CamaraVirtual, a digital ecosystem for strategic integration. It is not a static directory or a simple association: it is a living network where members, companies, and institutions discover each other, trade, and execute real projects with transparent governance and artificial intelligence.

From registration to your first business match in under 5 minutes. Artificial intelligence connects supply and demand, computes trust indices, and simulates the viability of each project before committing real resources.

The Member Dashboard

Nine integrated modules to discover, connect, and close deals within the network.

Dashboard

Your control center: active members, ongoing projects, open requests, and the network indices (Gini, Trust, HCI) in real time.

Inbox

Threaded internal messaging between members, to coordinate deals, quotes, and invitations without leaving the platform.

My Profile

Your verified identity: company, sector, and region, with your Trust Score computed by the TrustRank algorithm.

Directory

Browse every member of the network, filtering by sector and region. Find verified partners, suppliers, and allies.

AI Match

Type what you need in natural language and the AI connects you with the most compatible partners, ranked by affinity.

My Searches

Save your smart searches and receive new candidates automatically as the network grows.

Projects

Propose or join projects: role-based team assembly, milestones, documents, and Monte Carlo viability simulation before committing resources.

Invitations

Receive and manage invitations to roles within projects. Accept to join the team in a single click.

Exchange

The commercial marketplace of the network: post requests for quotation (RFQs) and respond to others to close deals.
