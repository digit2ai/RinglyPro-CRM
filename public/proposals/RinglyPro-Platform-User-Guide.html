3 Quick Start (5-Minute Setup)

Get up and running immediately

Log in to CW Carriers

Navigate to /cw_carriers/login. Enter your admin email and password. You'll land on the Dashboard showing today's open loads, active carriers, and recent calls.

Create your first load

Go to Loads in the sidebar. Click "+ New Load". Enter origin, destination, freight type, pickup date, and rates. Click Create. Your load appears on the load board as "Open".

Find carriers with AI matching

Click the "Find" button on your open load, or navigate to Carrier Matching. Enter the Load ID and click "Find Best Carriers". The AI scores and ranks the best carrier matches.

Launch a coverage campaign

Click "Launch Campaign" to send the load to your top matched carriers simultaneously. Rachel (voice AI) can automatically call carriers to secure coverage.

Track & deliver

Once a carrier accepts, the load moves to "Covered". Use Check Calls to monitor transit. Mark as "Delivered" when complete. Use Billing to auto-generate invoices.

Demo Data

Need sample data to explore? Go to Demo Data in the CW Carriers sidebar to generate sample loads, carriers, and calls instantly.

9 Billing & Invoicing

CW CARRIERS Dual-sided invoicing with POD tracking

Understanding AR vs AP

Accounts Receivable (AR)

Shipper Invoices

What the shipper owes you. Created at the sell rate. Track: Draft → Sent → Paid (or Overdue).

Accounts Payable (AP)

Carrier Payments

What you owe the carrier. Created at the buy rate. Track: Draft → Sent → Paid (or Overdue).

Auto-Generate Invoices

The fastest way to create invoices: enter a delivered Load ID in the Auto-Generate field. The system creates both the shipper invoice (AR) and carrier payment (AP) automatically with the correct amounts from the load record.

Invoice Lifecycle

1
Draft
Created, not sent
2
Sent
Invoice delivered
3
Paid
Payment received

If a sent invoice passes its due date without payment, it automatically shows as Overdue.

10 AI Carrier & Load Matching

CW CARRIERS Intelligent scoring to find the best carrier for every load

Carrier Matching

Navigate to Carrier Matching (under AI Brokerage). Enter a Load ID and click "Find Best Carriers". The AI scores carriers on six factors:

FactorWeightWhat It Measures
Lane History25%Has this carrier hauled this lane before?
Equipment Match20%Does the carrier have the right trailer type?
Reliability20%On-time %, acceptance rate, claims history
Rate Fit15%Is the carrier's rate within your budget?
Proximity10%How close is the carrier to the pickup?
Recency10%How recently has the carrier hauled for you?

Scores range from 0-100. 70+ = excellent match, 40-69 = good match, <40 = needs review.

Load-to-Load Matching

Navigate to Load Matching. Enter an anchor Load ID to find combinable loads:

  • Backhaul — Return trip that eliminates empty miles
  • Chain — Sequential loads forming a continuous route
  • Round Trip — Both legs of the circuit covered with freight
  • Relay — Long haul split into segments for regional carriers
Tip

Backhaul matches are the quickest wins. A carrier who just delivered in Dallas and needs to get back to Houston is likely to accept a lower rate, improving your margin.

11 Rate Intelligence

CW CARRIERS AI-powered pricing with confidence scoring

Get a Rate Quote

Navigate to Rate Intelligence. Enter origin, destination, equipment type, and optionally pickup date and miles. Click "Get Rate Recommendation".

The response includes:

  • Suggested Buy Rate — what to offer the carrier
  • Suggested Sell Rate — what to charge the shipper
  • Rate Per Mile — RPM for the lane
  • Margin % — recommended profit margin
  • Confidence Band — low/high range for buy and sell rates
  • Pricing Method — how the rate was calculated (see below)
  • Rationale — explanation of the data behind the recommendation

Pricing Methods (Tiered Fallback)

MethodConfidenceWhen Used
Exact Lane HistoryVery HighYou've hauled this exact city-to-city lane in the last 90 days
State CorridorHighNo exact city match, but state-to-state data available
Benchmark DataHighDAT/TruckStop/GreenScreens market rates available
Legacy DataMediumOlder historical data from CW Carriers load history
National AverageLowNo lane-specific data; uses national avg RPM × estimated miles

Lane Analysis

Use the Lane Analysis tab to study a lane's historical performance over the last 90 days (or custom period). See volume, average rate, min/max, competitor benchmarks, and trend direction.

14 HubSpot Integration

CW CARRIERS Bi-directional CRM sync

Setup (One-Time)

Get your HubSpot Private App token

In HubSpot: Settings → Integrations → Private Apps → Create. Grant scopes for contacts, deals, and activities. Copy the access token.

Configure in CW Carriers Settings

Go to Settings → HubSpot API tab. Paste your access token, enter Portal ID and Deal Pipeline ID.

Map deal stages

Configure which HubSpot pipeline stages correspond to load statuses: Open → Appointment Scheduled, Covered → Qualified to Buy, Delivered → Closed Won.

Enable auto-sync

Toggle on: Auto-sync Contacts, Auto-sync Deals, Auto-sync Call Activities. Click "Test Connection" to verify, then "Save Settings".

What Syncs

All sync events are logged in cw_hubspot_sync for audit trail. Check the HubSpot Sync page to review sync status and troubleshoot failures.

15 Logistics Command Center

LOGISTICS Instant-value tools for carriers and warehouse clients

The Logistics Dashboard at /logistics/dashboard provides four instant-use tools organized by vertical:

Transportation Tools

Lane Rate Lookup

Instant Market Rates

How to use: Select an origin city, destination city, and equipment type (Dry Van, Reefer, Flatbed, Step Deck). Click "Get Rate Estimate". The system queries historical load data and returns buy rate, sell rate, RPM, margin, and confidence band with rationale.

FMCSA Quick Check

Carrier Compliance

How to use: Enter an MC Number or DOT Number (or both). Click "Verify Carrier". Instantly see operating status, entity type, power units, drivers, safety rating, risk level, and insurance status (BIPD + Cargo). Green/red indicators for active/inactive.

Intralogistics Tools

Inventory Health Calculator

CSV Upload Analysis

How to use: Click the upload zone and select a CSV, JSON, or TSV file with inventory data. The system auto-detects quantity and date columns, then shows: total SKUs, total units, dead stock % (items with no movement >90 days), estimated turnover rate, and ABC classification (A/B/C bar chart).

Required columns: A quantity column (qty, stock, on_hand, etc.) and optionally a date column (last_movement, last_moved, etc.)

Warehouse Efficiency Score

Benchmark Assessment

How to use: Fill in your warehouse metrics: Active SKUs, Picks/Day, Error Rate %, Dock Doors, Avg Dwell Time (hours), Space Utilization %. Click "Calculate Score". Get a 0-100 score with letter grade (A-F), compared against industry benchmarks (avg: 62, top 25%: 78, best: 90), with specific notes on strengths and improvement areas.

Platform Status

At the bottom, two cards show live health status of CW Carriers and PINAXIS with green/red indicators. Click either card to navigate to that platform.

18 Workflow: Load to Delivery

CW CARRIERS End-to-end freight management in 8 steps

Complete Load Lifecycle

Create the load

Loads → "+ New Load" → enter origin, destination, freight type, rates, pickup date → Create.

Get a rate recommendation

Rate Intelligence → enter the lane → "Get Rate Recommendation" → use suggested buy/sell rates to set your margin.

Find carriers

Click "Find" on the load → Carrier Matching scores and ranks the best carriers → review top matches (aim for 70+ score).

Carrier Offers → review incoming offers → Counter or Accept → load status moves to "Covered".

Mark load as "In Transit" on pickup → Check Calls → use "Rachel Auto-Call" for automated driver status updates → "Notify Shipper" as needed.

Confirm delivery

Mark load as "Delivered" when freight arrives → Rachel confirms POD status automatically.

Invoice

Billing → "Auto-Generate" with the delivered Load ID → system creates shipper invoice (AR) + carrier payment (AP) → track to Paid.

20 Tips & Best Practices

Get the most out of the platform

Efficiency

Use the NLP Assistant

Instead of clicking through menus, type commands like "Show all open loads" or "Who are our top carriers this month?" in the NLP Assistant. Faster than navigating.

Automation

Let Rachel Handle Check Calls

Use "Rachel Auto-Call Driver" for every check call. She captures location, ETA, and status automatically. This ensures consistent shipper communication.

Revenue

Always Check Rate Intelligence

Before quoting a shipper, run the lane through Rate Intelligence. You might be leaving money on the table. Use the confidence band to set competitive but profitable rates.

Utilization

Run Load Matching Daily

Check Load Matching for backhaul opportunities on every anchor load. Even a 50% match score backhaul beats an empty return. This directly improves carrier margins.

Compliance

FMCSA Check Before Booking

Always verify carrier authority before booking. Use the FMCSA Quick Check on the Logistics dashboard or the FMCSA Compliance page in CW Carriers. Catch expired insurance before it's a problem.

CRM

Enable HubSpot Auto-Sync

Set up the HubSpot integration in Settings. Once enabled, contacts, deals, and call activities sync automatically. No more manual CRM data entry.

Billing

Auto-Generate Invoices on Delivery

As soon as a load is marked Delivered, use Billing → Auto-Generate. Creates both AR and AP invoices instantly. Faster billing = faster payment.

Analytics

Review Analytics Weekly

Check the Coverage Funnel and Carrier Performance weekly. Declining coverage rates or carrier reliability scores are early warning signs that need attention.

Warehouse

Use Both Inventory Tools

Upload inventory data to both the Logistics Inventory Health Calculator (quick check) and PINAXIS (deep analysis). The quick tool gives instant results; PINAXIS provides detailed recommendations.

Data

Bulk Import via Data Ingestion

Don't enter loads one-by-one. Export from your TMS as CSV and upload via Data Ingestion. The system auto-maps columns and imports in bulk. Works for loads, carriers, customers, and rates.

Webhooks

Connect Your WMS

In Settings → Inbound Webhook, configure your warehouse system to push new loads and status updates directly to CW Carriers. Eliminates manual load entry from warehouse operations.

Sales

Token Estimator + Contract Builder

When selling AI services to clients: model costs in Token Estimator, then link the estimate to Contract Builder for instant ESA generation with accurate pricing. Complete the sales cycle in minutes.
