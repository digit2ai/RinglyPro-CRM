Critical Gap Analysis

Seven Gaps Where Intuitive's Data Stops and MSK Intelligence Starts

Each gap represents a strategic vulnerability where competitors are already building advantage — and a capability MSK Intelligence has in production today.

1
Zero Pre-Operative Patient Intelligence
Surgeons arrive at the da Vinci console with plans developed entirely outside Intuitive's ecosystem. No patient triage, no surgical candidacy assessment, no risk stratification, no ROM baseline measurement. Stryker Mako already offers CT-based 3D pre-operative planning. Intuitive offers nothing.
MSK Intelligence Solution

AI-powered patient triage with 200-point urgency scoring, MediaPipe ROM baseline measurement before surgery, surgical candidacy assessment, and predictive outcome modeling. All through MCP tools: msk_create_case and msk_triage_case deliver structured pre-op intelligence directly into the surgical workflow.

2
Zero Post-Operative Outcome Tracking
Case Insights reviews surgical technique (video and kinematics) but tracks zero patient outcomes after the procedure. No readmission tracking, no complication monitoring, no functional recovery measurement. The richest surgical data in the world tells Intuitive nothing about whether the patient actually got better.
MSK Intelligence Solution

Automated outcome tracking via msk_prom_submissions, ROM recovery measurement over time, complication detection, and readmission risk scoring. The data that proves surgical value — the data hospitals and payers increasingly demand.

3
Zero Rehabilitation and ROM Monitoring
Intuitive's data stops when the patient leaves the OR. No rehabilitation protocols, no ROM tracking, no recovery milestones, no physical therapy integration. Zimmer Biomet's mymobility already tracks patient recovery via Apple Watch for ROSA procedures.
MSK Intelligence Solution

MediaPipe-powered ROM assessment through any browser camera. Rehabilitation progress tracking, PT protocol compliance, recovery benchmarking against population norms. Home exercise programs via msk_rehab with adherence monitoring. No hardware required — leapfrogs mymobility's Apple Watch dependency.

4
Zero Patient-Facing Engagement
My Intuitive is surgeon- and hospital-facing only. There is no patient portal, no patient app, no patient communication platform. In an era where patient experience drives hospital ratings, reimbursement, and referrals — Intuitive is invisible to the patient.
MSK Intelligence Solution

Patient-facing engagement platform with secure messaging, appointment scheduling, voice AI intake, symptom reporting, educational content delivery, and care plan adherence tracking. Available from any browser — no app download required.

5
No Longitudinal Data Continuity
Intuitive captures extraordinary intraoperative data — but it exists in isolation. There is no connection between pre-operative patient status, intraoperative technique, and post-operative outcomes. The surgical data island cannot answer the question that matters most: "Did this surgical approach produce better outcomes?"
MSK Intelligence Solution

MCP architecture connects pre-op assessment → intraoperative data → post-op outcomes → rehabilitation into a single longitudinal patient record. For the first time, Intuitive can correlate surgical technique with functional recovery — across 11,106 installed systems.

6
No Value-Based Care Reporting
Hospitals increasingly must demonstrate surgical value (outcomes per dollar) to payers. Intuitive can show utilization metrics — how many procedures, how much instrument usage — but cannot demonstrate outcomes tied to cost. This makes it harder for hospital administrators to justify the $2M+ system investment.
MSK Intelligence Solution

Surgical ROI dashboards via msk_get_dashboard_stats: outcome benchmarking, recovery timeline comparison (robotic vs. traditional), payer-ready quality measure reports. The data that turns a $2M capital expense into a justified investment with measurable patient impact.

7
No Standardized EHR Interoperability
Intuitive Hub integrates with hospital EMRs only through custom, hospital-by-hospital agreements. No documented FHIR API. No standardized HL7 interface. In a market where 79% of healthcare vendors support FHIR and the 21st Century Cures Act mandates interoperability with penalties up to $1M per violation — this is a compliance and competitive risk.
MSK Intelligence Solution

Full FHIR R4 compliance module providing standardized data exchange between Intuitive systems and any hospital EHR — Epic, Cerner, MEDITECH, or otherwise. MCP serves as the universal middleware: one protocol, every hospital, zero custom integration.
