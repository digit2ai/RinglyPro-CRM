As a RinglyPro partner, you must adhere to these marketing guidelines and branding standards to maintain your partnership status and ensure compliance with federal regulations (FTC, CAN-SPAM, GDPR). Violations may result in commission forfeiture or partnership termination.

1. Regulatory Compliance

1.1 FTC Disclosure Requirements

‚úÖ REQUIRED: Affiliate Disclosure

You MUST clearly disclose your affiliate/partner relationship when promoting RinglyPro.

‚úì DO - Proper Disclosures:

  • "I'm a RinglyPro partner and may earn a commission if you sign up through my link."
  • "Disclosure: This is an affiliate link. I earn from qualifying referrals."
  • "Partner disclosure: I have a financial relationship with RinglyPro."

‚úó DON'T - Hidden Partnerships:

  • Promoting without disclosure
  • Burying disclosure in fine print or footnotes
  • Using vague language like "I might benefit"

1.2 CAN-SPAM Act Compliance

Email Marketing Requirements:

  • Subject Lines: Must accurately reflect email content (no deceptive subjects)
  • From Address: Must identify who the email is from
  • Unsubscribe: Include clear opt-out mechanism in every email
  • Physical Address: Include your valid physical postal address
  • Honor Opt-Outs: Process unsubscribe requests within 10 business days

1.3 GDPR Compliance (EU/UK)

For European Audiences:

  • Obtain explicit consent before collecting email addresses
  • Provide clear privacy policy and data usage information
  • Allow users to access, correct, or delete their data
  • Use opt-in checkboxes (not pre-checked)

2. Prohibited Marketing Practices

üö´ STRICTLY FORBIDDEN

  • Spam: Unsolicited bulk emails, social media messages, or comments
  • False Claims: Exaggerated features, fake testimonials, or misleading guarantees
  • Brand Bidding: Bidding on "RinglyPro" or related trademarks in PPC campaigns
  • Cookie Stuffing: Forcing cookies without user interaction
  • Self-Referrals: Signing up fake accounts to earn commissions
  • Income Claims: Promising specific earnings ("Make $10,000/month!")
  • Trademark Misuse: Modifying RinglyPro logos or creating fake endorsements
  • Deceptive URLs: Using misleading domain names (e.g., ringlypro-official-discount.com)

3.2 Logo Usage

‚úì ALLOWED Logo Usage:

  • Use official RinglyPro logo provided in partner dashboard
  • Maintain minimum clear space around logo (equal to logo height)
  • Scale proportionally (do not stretch or distort)
  • Place on white, light gray, or navy backgrounds only

‚úó PROHIBITED Logo Usage:
