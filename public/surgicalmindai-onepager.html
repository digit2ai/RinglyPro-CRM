The Problem
Four breakdowns every capital sales team lives with today
Problem 01
Inconsistent Business Plans
Quality varies wildly across reps. Plans take weeks to assemble. The moment a hospital CFO spots numbers that don’t reconcile, credibility — and the deal — is gone.
Problem 02
Fragmented Clinician Data
Surgeon and physician volume commitments live in emails, paper notes, and survey tools. Nothing flows cleanly into the plan. Manual transcription introduces errors managers have to defend.
Problem 03
Unquantified Clinical Value
Peer-reviewed evidence proves your technology delivers superior outcomes. None of that value reaches the ROI model. The CFO sees a system price; the clinical advantage is missing from the math.
Problem 04
Zero Post-Placement Tracking
Once the system is placed, the plan dies. No proof the original case was right, no ongoing ROI story, and no reason for the hospital to expand the footprint or buy the next one.
