03 -- THE INVISIBLE EMPLOYEE
RACHEL: YOUR AI FREIGHT BROKER
Rachel is not a chatbot. She's a full-time freight broker who happens to be AI. She calls carriers, negotiates rates, books loads, tracks shipments, and updates everything -- by voice.
OUTBOUND -- BOOK CARRIERS
Calls top-ranked carriers for open loads
Quotes rate, answers questions
Handles objections with market data
Collects driver name, truck #
Sends rate confirmation
TRACKING -- CHECK CALLS
Calls driver every 4 hours
Asks location, ETA, issues
Logs updates to system in real-time
Notifies shipper proactively
Confirms delivery + POD
INBOUND -- HANDLE CALLS
Carrier calls in about a posted load
Shipper calls with new freight
Driver calls with delay or issue
Escalates to human when needed
Logs everything to CRM + TMS

RACHEL
Hi, this is Rachel from CW Carriers. We have a load, Miami to Atlanta, dry van, 42,000 lbs, picking up Thursday. Rate is $1,850. Can you cover it?
CARRIER
What's the pickup window?
RACHEL
Between 8am and noon at the Publix DC in Miami. Dock appointment required.
CARRIER
Rate is a little low. Can you do $2,000?
RACHEL
Our market data shows this lane averaging $2.79 per mile. I can go to $1,925 -- that's my best on this one.
CARRIER
OK, I'll take it.
RACHEL
Great. I'm sending the rate con now. Can I get the driver name and truck number?
CARRIER
Juan, truck 4521.
SYSTEM
Load booked. Rate con RC-8001 sent. Driver: Juan, Truck #4521. Status: COVERED. Shipper notified.
RACHEL CHECK CALL -- IN TRANSIT
RACHEL
Hi Juan, Rachel from CW. Checking in on load CW-8001, Miami to Atlanta. Are you loaded?
DRIVER
Yeah, loaded at 9am. Heading north on I-95.
RACHEL
Perfect. What's your ETA to Atlanta?
DRIVER
About 10 hours, should be there by 7pm.
RACHEL
Got it. I'll check back in 4 hours. Drive safe.
SYSTEM
Check call logged. Location: I-95 NB. ETA: 7:00 PM. Next call: 4hrs. Shipper notified.
RACHEL CONFIRMS DELIVERY
DRIVER
Hey Rachel, I just delivered in Atlanta.
RACHEL
Great. Any issues at the dock? Detention time?
DRIVER
Nope, clean delivery. Took about 30 minutes to unload.
RACHEL
Perfect. POD received. Your payment of $1,925 will process in 30 days. Thanks Juan.

Status: DELIVERED. Invoice INV-8001 ($2,450) sent to shipper. Carrier payment PAY-8001 ($1,925) queued. Profit: $525 (21.4%).
